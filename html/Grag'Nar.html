Grag'Nar

GragNar returned from hunting to find her clan utterly destroyed. As she mourned, a surviving enemy soldier shot an arrow into her eye. After killing him she found the body of her dead mother, reverently retrieved her seer stone, a glass eye, and placed it into her empty socket. Carrying the legacy of her clan, GragNar turned to bounty hunting. In her long years of hunting the parasites and predators of more civilized society, she has become a truly fearsome warrior.
