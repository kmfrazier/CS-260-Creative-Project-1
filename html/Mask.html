Mask

A shapeshifter who ran away from home as a child, Mask came into their own under the watchful eye of Nair, a master con artist, and became a skilled charlatan. To compartmentalize the trauma experienced in the Northern Wars, Mask developed an addiction to moon sugar, soon to be replaced by associative identity disorder. Over time, Mask grew into their role as a compulsive liar and con artist through a strict commitment to method acting, in part to distance from their own past.
